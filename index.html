Welcome to Hope for Whiskers! Whether you’re an active rescuer or someone looking to learn something about the stray cat situation, I’m here to help. To learn more about me and the Hope for Whiskers project, go to the “Mission” tab. If you’re curious about how important it is to neuter and spay your cats, look around the “Statistics” tab - the information there comes from reputable sources on the subject. If you need to reach out to share information about an abandoned animal near you, or if you need help finding a home for a recent rescue, reach out to me via the “Contact Us” tab.
